Hello! Welcome to my website.
This space is where I share my journey, combining my love for design, technology, and wellness.

I have over 6 years of experience as a product manager, bridging the gap between design, technology, and business.
I transformed business needs into product requirements, overseeing product design, testing, and implementation. I am exploring ways to apply my product experience to my personal passion for wellness.

I thrive not only in multi-desciplinary, but also in multi-cultural environment, having been born and raised in Tokyo and lived in the US, Mexico, and Spain. I enjoy working with diverse teams that value different cultures and beliefs.

Currently, I am a second-year MBA student at MIT Sloan School of Management.

Outside of the office/classroom, I love moving my body to beats and rhythms; I am an avid yoga practitioner and teacher (RYT 200), as well as a dancer (heels, hiphop, jazz dance).
